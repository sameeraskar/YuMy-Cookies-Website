About Us

You can find gluten-friendly cookies almost anywhere these days. You can find dairy-free cookies too. But both at the same time? That’s a little rarer. And ones that actually taste homemade? Practically non-existent. Until now!

YuMy Cookies has everything you want—and need. Gluten-friendly and dairy-free cookies that don’t just taste homemade… they are homemade!

Hi, I’m Maryam, the founder of YuMy Cookies—your new go-to cookie brand. YuMy Cookies is a small, local business, so we currently deliver within the Dallas Metroplex area, excluding Fort Worth.
